Walkthrough

There are skills that you learn very quickly while playing Zelda, but a lot of games have different controls, so it's hard to get used to. In the Walkthrough, I will explain how to do some pretty basic things that will help you during the game.

1) Basic Controls

The basic controls are moving, looking around, jumping, hitting, selecting, using the shield, shooting things(bow and arrow), throwing your weapon, parigliding, shield surfing, using ruins, and various ways of dodging.

Moving and Looking Around: It's obvious that this will be needed throughout the entire game. the left joystick is used to move around, and the right joystick is used to look around.
